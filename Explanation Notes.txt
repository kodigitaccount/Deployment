SaaS stands for Software as a Service.

It is a way of delivering software over the internet instead of installing it on your computer. 
You usually pay a monthly or yearly subscription and access the software through a web browser.

Examples of SaaS --> Google Workspace | Microsoft 365 | Salesforce | Zoom | Dropbox 

Instead of:  Buying software once Installing it on your device Managing updates yourself with SaaS:

The software is hosted by the provider | You access it online 

Think of Netflix for software: You don't own the movie files & You pay a subscription and use the service online.

SaaS works similarly for business software.
✅ Access from anywhere
✅ No installation needed
✅ Automatic updates
✅ Lower upfront cost
✅ Easy to scale as your business grows
